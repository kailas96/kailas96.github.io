Nagaraja

This idol is installed as a symbolic power representation of Lord Shiva and Lord Vishnu and in kashyapa sankalpa form

This Bhagavathi is known as Kannanagat Bhagavathi. ”Kannane kattiya Bhagavathi” which means Bhagavathi who has shown lord Krishna. Kannanagat Bhagavathi is also worshipped by Maniyani community.

NagaYakshi

NagaYakshi is in the center with Nagaraja on the right side and SarpaYakshi amma on the left side. Behind this temple Kartiyani Devi is installed looking towards western side and all these idols has been found to grant blessings to all those who come here..
