Brief on Kshetra

Kumaranallor Srinagaraja temple is one of the most serpent temple in Kerala.It was situated in Kumaranalloor ,a small village in Kottayam district.The Nagaraja temple was constructed and Punaprathishta during the year 1978.The first ThiruUtsav was held on the year 1979.The UpasanaMoorthi Nagaraja,Nagayekshi,Nagamathavu from Ullanur illom gave the devotees to the Tamil viswakarma for to bless the land forever with his eternal presence. The Madura illom has the right to do poojas in this temple and the Kadiyakkol illom has the right of thanthrika karmam.

Ohm Nagarajaya Nama:

Nagaraja is a Sanskrit word and its meaning is ‘King of Snakes’.Adishesha or Anantha and Vasuki are known as Nagarajas.Lord Anantha is the thousand-headed immortal and infinite snake upon whom rests Lord Vishnu in the primordial ocean of milk (Kshirasagara). The Lord of Serpents, Sheshanaga was incarnated to the earth in the form of Lakshmana during Treta Yuga and during the Dwapara Yuga, he incarnated as Balarama. He is said to be an eternal companion of Vishnu in all incarnations.Lord Vasuki is the necklace of Lord Shiva and he represents the dormant energy, called Kundalini, the serpent power.Anantha,Vasuki,Takshaka,Padma,Mahapadma, Gulika,Shamghapala and Karkkodaka are the Ashtanagas(eight great snakes) described in Hindu Puranas. Kashyapa and Kadru are considered as the parents of all snakes.
